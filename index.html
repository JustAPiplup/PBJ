The Best Cripsy French Fries That You can make at home

Learn How to Make Simple and Tasty French Fries

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • Oil
  • Salt And Vinegar
  • And Any Seasoning you want for the Fries

Instructions

  1. First Grab Your Potatoes and cut them into french fries length strips
  2. After Cutting the fries, Rinse them in water for a few second (Max is 20seconds)
  3. Place them in a pot with cold water, vinegar, and salt
  4. Bring it to a boil on high heat then turn it down to low
  5. Drain Your Fries and let them sit out to dry for 5 minutes
  6. Next Fry Your Fries at 205 degrees
  7. Fry them all for 50 seconds
  8. Then take them out and let them rest for 30 minutes
  9. after fry them a second time at 205 degrees for 4 minutes
  10. Now you have the best Crispy French Fries, now the last thing to do is put some seasoning on them and enjoy
